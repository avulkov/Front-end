Trifon Marinov Ivanov

  • Trifon Ivanov - an idol for a whole generation.
  • 27 July 1965 - born in Gorna Lipnica, Veliko Tarnovo.
  • 1976 - goes to football school and starts his career as Central Forward
  • 1983 - starts his professional career in Etar (Veliko Tarnovo)
  • 1988-1990 - plays in FC CSKA (Sofia).Win the Championship of Bulgaria twice (1989,1990) and the Trophy of Bulgaria (1989)
  • 1990-1993 - makes a transfer to FC Real Betis (Spain)
  • 1994 - bronze medalist from FIFA World Cup
  • 1996 - Champion of Austria with the team of Austria (Wien) and rival of UEFA Cup Winners Cup final
  • 13 February 2016 - dies at his home from heart attack.
